Dual H Bridge Stepper Driver on a Breadboard

Date: 27.09.2024 Building a dual h bridge stepper driver with a 2 pin per h-bridge interface. Build following the blueprint of Lewis Loflin from youtube video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QNA4aOjkGVE&t=53s Pullup resistors are 1kR. Pulldown resistors are 100kR. Current limiting LED Resistors 470R. Using IRF630 NMOS, IRF9630 PMOS, 2N2222 NPN Transistors.
